OUR FACILITY

With considerable years of experience, Bayscom Energy Limited prides itself in bulk sales and distribution of petroleum products across the country. Some of our major facilities include but are not limited to our retail service stations, peddler trucks, haulage trucks and storage facilities within the country. We own and operate a number of retail stations across the Country, while equally weighing options for additional retail stations and investment in logistics infrastructure in anticipation of increased supply volumes and growing energy needs in the near future.

Bayscom Energy Limited has established a significant presence in the major retail hubs across Nigeria.
