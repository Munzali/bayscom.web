About us

BAYSCOM ENERGY LTD is a Nigerian Indigenous Oil and Gas Company that Commenced the business of Marketing and distribution of Petroleum Products in 2012, with its headquarters in Abuja, Nigeria. The Company was fully Incorporated as BAYSCOM ENERGY LTD in the year 2002 and is an integrated downstream oil and gas company. A prominent player in the Nigerian industry.

Our perations span across the downstream value chain which include Petroleum Product Storage, Haulage/Distribution and Retail Sales. Our primary products include Petrol (PMS), Diesel (AGO) and Kerosene (DPK).

Our Subsidiary Company MOFAD ENERGY SOLUTIONS deals in Engine oils. The key role of lubricants is to stall friction in the Engine and to temper down heat. Our Car Care Products are Available at our retail outlets Nation Wide.

Our Vision

To be an independent oil and gas company in Nigeria through Innovation and optimization. We aim to be the best in the Nigerian Oil and gas industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide oil and natural gas products to the Nigerian and west African Markets through our innovations and development using best practices that provide sustainable business growth.
